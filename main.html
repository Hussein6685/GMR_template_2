WE ARE RECYCLING TODAY.

FOR OUR WORLD OF TOMORROW.

Whether gold, silver, platinum, palladium or rhodium, our world needs precious metals! MAIREC specializes in the recovery of precious metals from various materials containing them.

OUR OBJECTIVES ARE

  1. Even more success for our customers
  2. Even more opportunities for the self-realization for our employees
  3. Even more opportunities for the next generation of technology

By following these objectives, we preserve valuable resources for future generations.

IN DOING SO, WE PURSUE OUR MISSION

RECYCLE

Under this term we collect and recycle car catalysts, industrial catalysts, electronic scrap, various sweeps and concentrates and other industrial waste. And we are developing new ways to save further valuable raw materials from destruction.

REFINE

All our efforts aim at these goals: to develop the most economically and ecologically appropriate recycling way for each material entrusted to us and to find the best possible solution for each task. To thereby ensure the highest possible added value for you, our employees and the world of tomorrow.

RENEW

By »Renew« we mean the constant readjustment and optimization of our company, our employees’ qualities and talents are the raw material for our future success. In order to achieve this, we question what already exists, continuously develop ourselves further and reinvent ourselves cyclically.
